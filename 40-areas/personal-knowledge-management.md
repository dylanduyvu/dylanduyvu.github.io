# Personal Knowledge Management

## Current Understanding

Dylan's PKM system should prioritize durable insights and new learnings over generic note accumulation. Raw material matters, but mainly as evidence for claims, decisions, patterns, and future retrieval.

## Useful Patterns

- Store once, surface many places through metadata, links, and sparse hubs.
- Keep folders based on lifecycle and note type, not sprawling topic taxonomy.
- Let agents handle classification, dedupe, hub updates, and Git commits.
