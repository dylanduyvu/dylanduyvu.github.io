## Summary

The CSV contains 32 sources with columns for name, URL, publisher/author, description, publication frequency, focus area, quality score, and one-line reason. Every listed source currently has `quality_score = 90`, so the score column does not distinguish relative priority inside this file.

The list clusters around:

- GPU-backed lending, residual value, and asset-based finance: American Compute, USD.AI, ABF Journal, Monitor Daily.
- GPU pricing, compute economics, and market data: SemiAnalysis, Silicon Data, SLYD, GPUnex.
- Neocloud debt, SPVs, and AI infrastructure deal structures: Dave Friedman's Substack, Semi Fundamental, Deep Quarry.
- Data center / energy / infrastructure finance: Electron Economics, Data Center Knowledge, DCD, Data Center Frontier, Infrastructure Investor.
- Operator and platform blogs: CoreWeave, Lambda, FluidStack, RunPod, Vast.ai.
- Bank / institutional research channels: Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, KBRA.
