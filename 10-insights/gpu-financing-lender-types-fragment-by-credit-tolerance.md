# GPU financing lender types fragment by credit tolerance

## Claim

GPU financing is not one lender market. It fragments across institutional private credit, boutique/cowboy private credit, crypto capital, banks, and equipment finance shops, each with different credit standards and tolerance for story-credit risk.

## Why It Matters

Customer discovery and product positioning should not say "lenders" generically. A residual-value, RVI, novation, or SLA-verification product may sell differently depending on whether the counterparty is a bank, lessor, equipment finance shop, private credit fund, or crypto-native lender.

## Evidence

- 2026-06-16: Bernie segmented GPU financing capital into established private credit, boutique/cowboy private credit, crypto lenders/whales, banks, and equipment finance shops.
- 2026-06-16: He described established private credit as institutional and higher bar, while smaller boutique private credit can be much more aggressive.
- 2026-06-16: He said banks have high criteria after Basel III and often need strong credit markers such as S&P ratings or substantial revenue.
- 2026-06-16: He described equipment finance shops as a separate category that finances equipment across industries and can range from story-credit-driven to institutional.
- 2026-06-16: He included USD.AI-style crypto/on-chain lenders and crypto whales as a distinct capital pool.

## Implications

- The same product should probably have different sales narratives by capital type.
- Banks may care about regulatory/accounting recognition; private credit may care about speed and downside protection; crypto lenders may care about deal flow and collateral monitoring.
- Intro requests should specify target profile, because Bernie said vague asks are harder to route.

## Counterpoints / Uncertainty

- This is Bernie's segmentation from his sales surface, not a full market map.
- Categories may overlap; some equipment finance shops are bank-affiliated, and some private credit funds behave like equipment lenders.
- The transcript does not quantify relative deal volume by segment.
