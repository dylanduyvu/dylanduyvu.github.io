# CoreWeave

## Context

CoreWeave is a major neocloud / GPU infrastructure company. In the current vault, it matters less as a target account and more as a reference case for compute commoditization, GPU financing, residual value, and neocloud valuation framing.

## Relationship

No direct relationship captured in the vault yet.

## Useful Signals

- Brannin McBee argues compute is not yet fungible enough to trade like a commodity because goodput, MFU, and operational reality differ across providers.
- CoreWeave's DGX reference-spec framing may provide a compute grade, while goodput, MFU, SLA, and contractual terms become basis.
- Per Friedman, the non-commodity framing supports a valuation bundle around operator moat, contracted cash flows, GPU useful life, and cost of capital.
