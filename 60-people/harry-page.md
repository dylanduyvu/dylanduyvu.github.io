# Harry Page

## Context

Harry Page is a USD.AI contact who provided lender-side customer discovery on GPU-backed lending, offtake contracts, GPU collateral, and operator performance risk.

## Relationship

Dylan connected through Sam and spoke with Harry / the USD.AI team on 2026-06-29. Harry was open to keeping in touch and bouncing ideas back and forth.

## Useful Signals

- He directly rejected lending against a bare contract without GPU collateral as a recovery asset.
- He said USD.AI has not seen customers offload committed long-term GPU capacity.
- He described supply scarcity, meaningful deposits, and future supply uncertainty as reasons customers hold onto committed capacity.
- He volunteered SLA/uptime performance verification as an active problem USD.AI is looking at.
- He described value warranties/reinsurance as a way USD.AI reduces loss-given-default on GPU hardware.
