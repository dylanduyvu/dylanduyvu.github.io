# Dylan Patel

## Context

Dylan Patel is the founder of SemiAnalysis (a semiconductor/AI-infrastructure research firm) and a recurring public analyst source for this vault on GPU/neocloud economics and AI infrastructure. Not to be confused with the vault owner, Dylan Vu.

## Relationship

No direct relationship captured; a public source whose podcast/writeup claims are logged here.

## Useful Signals

- Frames the neocloud risk as the balance sheet / contract book rather than GPU performance (CoreWeave benchmarks above hyperscalers; the fragility is financing pre-sold, debt-backed builds).
- Claims Tinker (Thinking Machines) does a few hundred million ARR, the highest known revenue of any of the ~75 "neolabs" (unverified).
- Argues co-design (silicon + kernels + model) yields ~100x, not the 8x of improving each alone.
- Reports Anthropic runs >80% gross margin on Opus 4.8 tokens and is profitable ex-SBC in Q2 2026.
- Argues Jensen Huang funds neoclouds/neolabs on purpose to weaken Google TPU and Amazon Trainium over ~5 years.
