# Brannin McBee

## Context

Brannin McBee is a CoreWeave co-founder. In Dave Friedman's 2026-06-11 article, McBee is the source of the "no-for-now, maybe-later" argument that compute is not yet fungible enough to trade like a commodity.

## Relationship

No direct relationship captured in the vault yet.

## Useful Signals

- He frames compute non-fungibility through differences in goodput, MFU, and operational reality across providers.
- He says CoreWeave builds to DGX reference spec.
- Per Friedman, his background in natural gas, power, and agricultural trading makes the non-commodity framing strategically meaningful rather than naive.
